**Neon Divide** is a high-octane, retro-futuristic arcade game inspired by the classics like *Qix* and *Volfied*, but injected with a lethal dose of Cyberpunk adrenaline. Navigate the digital void, carve out your territory, and dodge the firewall defenses trying to purge your existence.

Built with modern web technologies to deliver a seamless, app-like experience in the browser.

## üïπÔ∏è Gameplay Loops

- **Territory Capture:** Draw lines to claim space. Close the shape to capture.
- **Roguelike Progression:** Earn credits, upgrade your speed, shield, and dash cooldowns between runs.
- **VHS Aesthetics:** CRT filters, chromatic aberration, and retro-wave vibes.
- **Leaderboard:** Compete for the high score in the global network.
